Common Questions

Answers to what you're already thinking.

How is Avalanche Growth different from other AI consultants?

Most AI consultants deliver a strategy deck and leave. We stay through the entire implementation — tool setup, integration, and staff training — and don't consider the job done until your team is independently running AI workflows. We measure ourselves by your adoption rate and ROI, not by deliverable count.

What size company do you typically work with?

We work with international businesses from 10 to 500 employees across SaaS, fintech, e-commerce, professional services, and more. The sweet spot is companies generating $1M–$50M in revenue that are ready to systematically scale with AI.

Do you work with specific AI tools or are you tool-agnostic?

We are completely tool-agnostic. We recommend the right tools for your specific use case, budget, and existing tech stack — not the ones we get referral fees from. We work across the full landscape: OpenAI, Anthropic Claude, Google Gemini, Microsoft Copilot, Zapier, Make, n8n, HubSpot AI, Notion AI, and many others.

What does the discovery call involve?

It's a free 45-minute call where we learn about your business, your current tech stack, your team structure, and where you feel the most pain. We'll give you honest feedback on where AI can help most — and where it might not be worth the investment. No sales pressure, no pitch deck.

How long before we see results?

Most clients see their first measurable improvement within 30 days of starting implementation — typically in one high-impact workflow we prioritise first. Full ROI is usually visible by month 3. We build a quick-win roadmap specifically to ensure you see results before the full engagement ends.

What if our team is resistant to AI adoption?

This is actually the most common challenge — and the one we're best equipped to solve. Our training methodology is role-specific and practical, showing each team member exactly how AI helps them personally. Adoption resistance comes from abstract training. We make it concrete and immediately useful.
